Our story:

College Falafel circa winter 2016

Everything is made from scratch, nothing comes prepackaged.

At College Falafel we are known for our shawarmas, falafels, and East Coast Donair. We are one of the first restaurants for Halifax Style Donairs in Toronto. We began to carry the east coast donair in 2004, just 1 year after it was introduced.
There is a variety of vegan and vegetarian foods at College Falafel, the most popular one being our falafel. One of our main goals is about making homemade foods right in front of you, our slogan is “home made from scratch” because that is what matters to us.

College Falafel has been a catering service for all types of events, birthdays, schools, weddings, corporations, institutions, and many more.

College falafel is a place of comfort and warmth where everyone is welcome and is treated with respect and attention. With a combined 70 years of food industry experience Oljana and Bardhyl, have dedicated their lives to making and serving great culinary experiences.
